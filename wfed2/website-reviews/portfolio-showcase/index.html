2023 NWTC Student Showcase
Digital Arts Festival

The NWTC 2023 Digital Arts Student Showcase highlights the work of Digital Art students at Northeast Wisconsin Technical College. On campus showcase reception will be held December 7th from 4:30-6:30pm in the NWTC College of Business - Digital Arts Atrium.

Digital Art comprises of our audio, design & graphic, print, photography, video, web design and digital media programs. Check out the latest creations of our talented students. Enjoy!
